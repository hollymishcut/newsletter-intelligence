Consumer video gen is dead (Sora's numbers prove it). Enterprise use cases — training data synthesis, medical imaging, security/surveillance, manufacturing QA — are massively underserved by current video models.
Physical AI Simulation Layer
Mind Robotics and others accelerating sim-to-real training. The simulation software/infrastructure layer (like Unity was for gaming) for physical AI training is wide open. Massive TAM, technical moat, early innings.
PM × AI Upskilling Platforms
With 7,300+ PM openings and AI reshaping every product role, structured training for AI-native product management is an underserved market. Enterprise-focused, not Udemy-style consumer.

Enterprise AI Capability Building

Design-Code Integration Layer
